Company

Kaplan Asset Holdings (“KAH”) is a research-driven treasury focused on acquiring and holding Sui (SUI) as a long-term corporate reserve. We operate with institutional controls, clear policy, and transparent reporting.

Overview

KAH manages treasury capital in GBP with a mandate to accumulate and safeguard SUI. Governance emphasises operational simplicity, segregation of duties, and continuity planning across custody and reporting.

Domicile: UK Functional currency: GBP Focus: Sui ecosystem Reporting: monthly
Corporate strategy
Core position

Maintain a long-duration allocation to SUI as digital treasury reserve.

Accumulation

Deploy GBP on a multi-cycle basis with disciplined sizing and pacing.

Simplicity

Minimise protocol risk and operational complexity; favour clear controls.

Treasury policy
Holding policy

SUI held in segregated wallets with documented signing and recovery procedures.

Pricing policy

Purchases recorded at GBP consideration paid; period-end fair value disclosed.

Liquidity

Maintain sufficient GBP runway to meet operating expenses for 12 months.

Risk management
Market

Primary exposure is SUI/GBP volatility; sizing and cadence reflect this.

Custody

Hardware keys, multi-sig controls, restricted withdrawals, and access reviews.

Operational

Change control for wallets, vendors, and data sources; audit trails retained.

Accounting

Internally, SUI holdings are presented in GBP at period-end fair value with separate tracking of acquisition cost and weighted average unit cost. Realised gains/losses are recognised on disposal; remeasurement is disclosed for transparency.

Reporting
  • Overview dashboard — live SUI price, holdings, and NAV (GBP).
  • Purchases ledger — timestamped acquisitions with WAC and cumulative holdings.
