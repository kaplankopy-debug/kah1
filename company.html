Company

Kaplan Asset Holdings (“KAH”) is a research-driven treasury focused on acquiring and holding Sui (SUI) for long-term corporate strategy and liquidity management.

Overview

KAH manages treasury capital with a mandate to hold SUI as a strategic corporate reserve. We operate with institutional controls, independent oversight, and transparent reporting.

Domicile: UK Base currency: GBP Reporting: monthly Focus: Sui ecosystem

Corporate Strategy

  • Core position in SUI. Build and maintain a long-duration allocation to SUI as digital treasury reserve.
  • Disciplined accumulation. Add SUI opportunistically over cycles while managing GBP cash runway.
  • Operational simplicity. Minimise protocol risk, third-party exposure, and unnecessary complexity.

Treasury Policy

  • Holding policy. SUI held in segregated wallets with documented signing procedures.
  • Pricing policy. GBP functional currency. Purchases booked at GBP consideration paid; where applicable, fair-valued using reliable market data.
  • Liquidity. Maintain sufficient GBP to meet 12 months of operating expenses.
  • Counterparties. Use reputable exchanges/custodians with robust compliance and security standards.

Risk Management

  • Market risk. Exposure primarily to SUI price volatility vs GBP.
  • Custody risk. Multi-sig, hardware key management, and restricted withdrawal policies.
  • Operational risk. Change control for wallets, vendors, and data sources; periodic review.
  • Compliance. KYB/AML with exchanges and service providers; audit trail retention.

Accounting

For internal management reporting, SUI holdings are presented in GBP at period-end fair value with separate tracking of historical acquisition cost and weighted average unit cost. Realised gains/losses are recognised on disposal; unrealised remeasurement is disclosed for transparency.

Reporting

  • Overview dashboard. Live SUI price, holdings, and NAV in GBP.
  • Purchases ledger. Time-stamped SUI acquisitions with weighted average cost and cumulative holdings.
